## Voice Production Notes

- Accent: Educated Southern American accent
- Timbre: Warm baritone
- Delivery: Calm authority
- Pacing: Measured pacing
- Expression: Restrained emotional expression
- Diction: Clear diction
- Performance exclusions:
  - No promotional delivery
  - No theatrical delivery
- General mix note: Narration is primary. Ambient audio must support the voice and never compete with it.

## Complete Approved Narration Script

### Scene 1 - Foundation

- Time range: `0:00-0:05`
- Narration: None
- Pause guidance: No spoken narration. Leave the opening visual and ambient drone unvoiced for the full scene duration.

### Scene 2 - Emergence

- Time range: `0:05-0:12`
- Approved narration:

> Before systems are built... they are understood.

- Recommended pauses:
  - After `Before`: `0.2s`
  - At the ellipsis after `built...`: `0.8s`
  - Before the final word `understood.`: `0.2s`

### Scene 3 - System Formation

- Time range: `0:12-0:25`

### Scene 5 - Identity

## Session Guidance For Voice Generation Or Recording

- Preserve the approved wording exactly as written.
- Do not paraphrase punctuation-driven pauses.
- Treat ellipses as intentional reflective timing rather than hesitation.
- Maintain measured pacing across all lines.
- Keep emphasis controlled and architectural rather than dramatic.
- Allow silence to function as part of the presentation, especially in Scene 1 and after the final line in Scene 5.
