# Purpose

This document is the authoritative production storyboard for the BiblionOCR introduction video.

All production artifacts—including shot lists, narration packages, DaVinci Resolve manifests, asset assignments, production readiness reports, and future revisions—shall derive from this document.

No implementation tool or AI assistant should modify this storyboard during production. Any future revisions shall be made by updating this document and incrementing its revision number.

---

# Production Objectives

The introduction video shall:

* Introduce the philosophy of BiblionOCR.
* Present the project as a living, observable software architecture.
* Establish the visual identity of the project.
* Inspire curiosity rather than explain every feature.
* Remain suitable for GitHub, YouTube, Patreon, presentations, and future project documentation.

# Audio Design

Narration is primary.

Ambient audio supports narration.

Music shall never compete with spoken voice.

Silence is an intentional production element.

---

# Transition Rules

Use:

* Fade
* Cross dissolve

Avoid:

* Glitch effects
* Rapid cuts
* Flash transitions
* Excessive camera motion

The visual language should communicate clarity, structure, and intentional design.
